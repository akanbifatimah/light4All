ABOUT LIGHT 4 ALL

Light 4 All began in 2022 with a simple objective: "to deliver the most efficient and reliable platform to report electricity issues.

Our team of service professionals are interested in putting an end to electricity issues to reduce the use of fuel and rate at which the atmosphere is polluted with dangerous gases. So Light 4 All caters about welfare of citizens and the Ecosystem.

Our recent Achievements

Light 4 All has been able to solve electricity problem in some communities.

BAMIDELE JOSHUA-FROM OGHARA

Light 4 All, yesterday, said it has completed repairs on a 30MVA 132/33kV power transformer in its Oghara Transmission Substation, Delta State. The power transformer which was installed by Niger Delta Development Commission, NDDC, in September 2016, developed a fault on May 10, 2018, plunging parts of the state into darkness.
